A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1995, Delhi International Academy has been at the forefront of progressive education in India. Our commitment to academic rigor, character development, and global citizenship has made us the preferred choice for families seeking world-class education.

We offer internationally recognized curricula including CBSE, Cambridge International, and International Baccalaureate programs, ensuring our students are prepared for success in the global landscape.

Admissions

Join Our Academy

Begin your child's journey to academic excellence and global citizenship with our streamlined admission process.

1

Online Application

Complete our comprehensive online application form with required documents including previous academic records, birth certificate, and passport-size photographs.

2

Entrance Assessment

Participate in age-appropriate entrance assessment covering academic subjects, logical reasoning, and interactive sessions with our counselors.

3

Parent Interview

Meet with our admission committee to discuss your educational expectations, child's interests, and alignment with our school philosophy.

4

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 5-7 working days followed by fee payment and enrollment formalities to secure your child's seat.
